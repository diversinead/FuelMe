Weekly Fuelling Plan

Eat for the work, not the day · ~49 kg
Easy: 3–4 g/kg → 150–200 g carbs
Hard / long: 5–7 g/kg → 250–340 g carbs
Protein: 1.6–2.2 g/kg → 80–110 g target
Rule 01 Dodge the insulin dip — on key sessions eat 0–20 min pre or > 2 hrs pre. The 30–90 min gap is the trap.
Rule 02 Refuel within 30 min after AM sessions — carbs + protein, no exceptions.
Rule 03 Eat for the work — don't drop carbs on easy days, and pre-load the day before a long run.
