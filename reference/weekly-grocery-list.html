Dinner adds ~70–90 g carbs and ~35–40 g protein on top. Daily protein lands at ~115–150 g (2.3–3.0 g/kg) — well above the 1.6–1.8 g/kg endurance target.

Note 01 Up&Go 250 ml = ~18 g protein, ~21 g carbs. The 500 ml version = ~35 g protein, ~42 g carbs — one bottle hits the hard-day target.
Note 02 Buy Greek, not natural — plain Greek yoghurt sits at ~10 g protein per 100 g. Watery "natural" yoghurts can be half that. No need to chase the marketed "high protein" tubs.
Note 03 Batch-cook Sunday — rice + chicken + boil 6 eggs. Weekday lunches become 60 seconds of assembly.
Note 04 Frozen > fresh berries for smoothies — cheaper, no waste, makes the drink cold without ice.
